Behind The Scenes

From idea to publish

Step 1

Guest Identified

Tony sources a partner or practitioner with a proven, tactical story to tell.

→
Step 2

Recording Session

A focused conversation is recorded, covering real frameworks instead of scripted pitches.

→
Step 3

Edit & Categorize

Session is edited and tagged into one of seven categories: Affiliate, Publisher, Agency, Growth, Email, Tech Partner, or Customer AI Use Case.

→
Step 4

Publish & Promote

Episode goes live on everflow.io/masterclass and is promoted via newsletter, social, and partner channels.

Every episode is tagged by audience so reps can send the right episode to the right partner in seconds.
